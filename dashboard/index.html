Retention Strategy Recommendations
Champions · 18% of customers
Launch VIP Loyalty Program
These customers drive 52% of revenue and respond strongly to exclusivity. Offer early product access, free shipping thresholds, and personalised thank-you outreach. Focus on increasing basket size, not frequency — they already buy often.
Projected impact: +8% revenue from upsell
Loyal · 22% of customers
Personalised Loyalty Discounts
Send email campaigns with a 10% loyalty discount tied to their purchase history. Cross-sell complementary product categories — e.g. if they buy home décor, recommend seasonal gift sets. Aim to elevate them to Champion status.
Projected impact: 30% conversion to Champion
At Risk · 24% of customers
60-Day Win-Back Email Series
Trigger automated win-back emails when a customer crosses the 60-day inactivity threshold. Use urgency messaging ("Your exclusive offer expires in 48hrs") with a 15% time-limited discount. Two-touch sequence: Day 0 soft nudge, Day 7 urgency.
Projected impact: Reduce churn 18%
Lost · 36% of customers
Low-Budget SMS Reactivation
Run a single low-cost SMS/email blast with a significant reactivation offer (20% off). If no response after 2 attempts over 30 days, move to suppression list to reduce marketing spend waste. ROI focus over volume.
Projected impact: 5% reactivation rate
